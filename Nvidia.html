NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4090

A powerful and affordable GPU built for modern gaming and creative workloads.

Architecture and Design

The RTX 4090 is based on NVIDIA's Ada Lovelace architecture, offering great efficiency and performance improvements over previous generations.

Performance and Gaming

Designed for 1080p and 1440p gaming, the RTX 4090 delivers smooth frame rates, ray tracing, and DLSS 3 support for better visuals and performance.

Cooling and Power Efficiency

With low power consumption and advanced cooling solutions, the RTX 4090 stays cool and quiet even under load.

Connectivity and Features

Equipped with HDMI 2.1 and DisplayPort 1.4a, the card supports high refresh rate monitors and multi-display setups for gamers and creators alike.

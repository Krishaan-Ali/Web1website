Why AI is important

With AI adoption growing rapidly, discussions around ethics and regulation are becoming increasingly important. Governments and organizations are working to establish AI governance frameworks to address concerns such as bias, privacy, and accountability. EU AI Act: The European Union is leading the way in AI regulation with the AI Act, which categorizes AI systems based on risk levels and enforces strict compliance measures. US AI Executive Order: The Biden administration has introduced initiatives to ensure responsible AI development, emphasizing transparency and fairness. China’s AI Governance: China continues to develop AI with state-led oversight, focusing on aligning AI advancements with national interests.

Samsung Galaxy S25

The future of mobile technology, packed into Samsung's most advanced smartphone yet.

Design and Display

The Galaxy S25 features a refined, premium design crafted from recycled aluminum and Gorilla Glass Victus 3. Its edge-to-edge 6.9-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display supports a 144Hz refresh rate for buttery-smooth visuals and peak brightness of 2,800 nits for clear visibility even under direct sunlight. With ultra-thin bezels and a lightweight frame, it delivers a perfect balance between beauty and durability.

Performance and Hardware

Powered by the latest Snapdragon 8 Gen 4 or Exynos 2500 chipset (based on region), the Galaxy S25 delivers groundbreaking CPU and GPU performance. Backed by 12GB to 16GB of LPDDR5X RAM and UFS 4.0 storage, it handles multitasking, gaming, and AI-driven tasks with ease. Enhanced thermal management ensures sustained performance under heavy load, making it a beast for productivity and entertainment alike.

Camera and Imaging

The triple-lens camera system includes a 200MP wide-angle lens, a 50MP ultra-wide sensor, and a 10x periscope telephoto lens for professional-grade photography. With improved nightography, real-time AI scene detection, and 8K video recording at 30fps, the S25 captures stunning images in virtually any environment. The front camera also features auto-frame technology for crystal-clear selfies and group shots.

Battery Life and Charging

With a 5,500mAh battery, the Galaxy S25 provides all-day power, even with heavy usage. It supports 65W wired charging and 45W fast wireless charging, allowing you to reach 50% charge in under 20 minutes. AI-powered power optimization learns your usage habits to reduce background drain, while reverse wireless charging makes it easy to share power with your earbuds or another phone on the go.

Google Pixel 9 Pro

The Pixel 9 Pro combines advanced AI capabilities with refined hardware, offering a seamless and intelligent Android experience.

Design and Display

The Google Pixel 9 Pro features a premium matte aluminum frame with Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2 on both front and back. Its 6.7-inch OLED LTPO display delivers up to 120Hz refresh rate and peaks at 2000 nits brightness, making it ideal for both outdoor visibility and immersive content consumption.

Performance and Software

Powered by the custom Google Tensor G4 chip, the Pixel 9 Pro delivers smooth multitasking, responsive gaming, and advanced AI performance. With Android 15 out of the box, users benefit from longer software support and exclusive Pixel features like Magic Eraser, Live Translate, and Adaptive Battery enhancements.

Camera System

The Pixel 9 Pro boasts a triple-lens setup: a 50MP main sensor, 48MP ultra-wide, and 48MP telephoto with 5x optical zoom. Computational photography continues to shine, with Real Tone, Night Sight, and advanced HDR+ producing detailed, true-to-life shots in any condition. The front camera supports 4K video and ultra-wide framing for group selfies.

Battery and Charging

The device includes a 5050mAh battery with 30W fast wired charging and 23W wireless charging. Smart charging routines optimize battery health over time, while the Extreme Battery Saver mode can stretch usage up to 72 hours in critical scenarios.
